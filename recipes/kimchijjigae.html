Kimchi Jjigae

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Rice Water
  • Pickled Shrimp
  • Pork Belly
  • Scallions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Minced Garlic
  • (Optional) Korean Miso
  • (Optional) Medium Firm Tofu

Recipe

  1. In medium heat, put 400g of bite sized pork belly into the pot with 500ml Rice Water, 1 TBSP of shrimp paste, and 1 TBSP of Korean Miso
  2. Once pork belly is boiling, put in bite sized kimchi
  3. Season with Soysauce, Pickled Shrimp and Kimchi Juice, add some Garlic
  4. If you are putting in Tofu, this is the time!
  5. Let the soup simmer for 1 hour
  6. Add chopped scallions for garnish before serving
